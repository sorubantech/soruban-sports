# Competitor Analysis — Cricket Academy & Sports Management Software

**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Status:** Research Phase
**Data Source:** Based on market knowledge up to May 2025. Recommend verifying pricing and feature updates via live research.

---

## Executive Summary

The cricket academy management software market is fragmented. Most solutions fall into one of four categories:

1. **Cricket-specific scoring/community apps** (CricHeroes, CricClubs) — large user bases but weak on academy management
2. **Sports academy management platforms** (Classcard, SportsPlus, CoachNow) — generic, not cricket-tailored
3. **Cricket coaching/analysis tools** (PitchVision, Cricuru, StanceBeam) — focused on technique, not operations
4. **General-purpose ERP/management tools** adapted for sports (Omnify, FreshLearn)

**No single product combines all of:** structured curriculum, student lifecycle management, social feed, parent community, and cricket-specific coaching content — which is Soruban Sports' opportunity.

## MARKET INSIGHTS

### Market Size & Opportunity

- **India has an estimated 10,000-15,000+ cricket academies** (organized and semi-organized)
- Most are **single-location, run by ex-cricketers or passionate coaches** with 20-200 students
- The market is growing due to: IPL aspirations, parental willingness to invest in cricket coaching, increasing urbanization
- **Current digitization is near zero** — most academies run on WhatsApp + paper registers + cash
- The Indian SaaS market for sports/coaching is in its **very early stage** (<5% penetration)

### Key Market Pain Points (Validated by Competitors' Existence)

1. **No structured data** — Academies have no digital records of student progress
2. **WhatsApp chaos** — All communication on WhatsApp, nothing is organized or retrievable
3. **Revenue leakage** — Cash payments, no tracking, late fees not followed up
4. **No parent visibility** — Parents pay INR 3,000-10,000/month but have no idea what their child is learning
5. **Coach dependency** — When a coach leaves, all knowledge about students goes with them
6. **No standard curriculum** — Every academy teaches differently with no structure
7. **No scalability** — Expanding to a second branch means starting from scratch operationally

### What Competitors Are Missing (Soruban's Whitespace)

| Gap in Market | Soruban's Solution |
|---------------|-------------------|
| No structured cricket curriculum platform | Stage-wise curriculum with animated content library |
| No parent community/engagement beyond notifications | Parent cricket community, dual profiles, parent matches |
| No social feed replacing WhatsApp | Academy social feed with visibility levels |
| No staff knowledge retention | Staff communication layer with permanent notes |
| No data-driven player development | Assessment system + stage progression + certificates |
| No cross-academy benchmarking | Comparative benchmarking (anonymized) |
| No integrated solution (operations + coaching + community) | All-in-one platform |

## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### 1. Positioning
Position Soruban Sports as **"The Operating System for Cricket Academies"** — not just a management tool, not just a coaching app, but the complete platform that runs an academy's entire operation.

### 2. Differentiation Priorities
- **Animated content library** is the highest-value moat — no competitor has this
- **Parent cricket community** is unique — creates a network effect no competitor can match
- **Structured curriculum (autonomous college model)** is unprecedented in cricket

### 3. Competitive Strategy
- **Don't compete with CricHeroes** on match scoring — integrate with them instead
- **Beat Classcard** on operational features while adding cricket-specific depth they cannot
- **Beat CricketGraph** on everything — better UX, more features, stronger content
- **Ignore PitchVision/StanceBeam** for now — they serve a different segment (hardware/analysis)

### 4. Potential Partnerships
| Partner | Type | Value |
|---------|------|-------|
