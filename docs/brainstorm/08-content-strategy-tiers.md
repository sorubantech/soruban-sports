# Content Strategy — 3-Tier Approach

**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Status:** Proposed — For Discussion

---

## The Question

Should we create all animated videos before launch, or use existing internet content for MVP and build our own library over time?

---

## Decision: 3-Tier Content Strategy (Recommended)

### Tier 1: Curated Videos (MVP — Day 1)
- Handpick best YouTube/internet videos per skill
- Vetted by cricket expert for technique accuracy
- Embedded or linked (not downloaded — legal compliance)
- Labeled as "Curated Resource" — not Soruban branded content
- Cost: ₹0
- Effort: 1-2 days of curation per stage

### Tier 2: Academy's Own Content (MVP — Day 1)
- Academy coaches upload their own videos, drills, notes
- This is the "custom" part of the autonomous college model
- No cost to us — academy creates this content
- Gives academies ownership and personalization

### Tier 3: Soruban Originals (Month 3-6, Post-Launch)
- Branded 3D animated technique videos
- Created using Hybrid pipeline (real coach → AI motion capture → Blender → voiceover)
- Replaces Tier 1 curated videos gradually
- Becomes the competitive moat
- Funded by early revenue from paying academies

---

## Why This Approach Wins

| Benefit | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|
| **Faster launch** | Content doesn't block development — launch in 8-10 weeks |
| **Zero content cost at launch** | Save ₹3-5 lakhs upfront |
| **Validate before investing** | Prove academies want the platform before spending on content |
| **Revenue funds content** | Early paying customers fund the video production |
| **Progressive improvement** | Each Soruban Original replacement is a visible upgrade — academies see platform improving |
| **Lower risk** | If platform doesn't gain traction, we haven't sunk ₹5L into videos |

## Curation Guidelines for Tier 1

When selecting YouTube videos for each skill:

### Selection Criteria
- Technique must be correct (verified by cricket expert)
- Video quality: minimum 720p
- Clear demonstration — no cluttered background
- Coaching style (not just highlights/entertainment)
- English language (aligned with MVP decision)
- Duration: under 5 minutes (shorter is better)
- No inappropriate ads or content
- Creator is credible (coach, ex-player, established channel)

### Top YouTube Channels for Cricket Coaching Content
1. **Cricket Coaching with Neil** — Structured drills and technique
2. **CricketMentor** — Indian coaching focused
3. **VVS Laxman Cricket Academy** — Professional academy content

## Legal Compliance

| Action | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Use YouTube official embed (iframe) only | Required |
| Never download or rehost videos | Required |
| Credit creator name and channel | Required |
| Label as "Curated External Resource" | Required |
| Reach out to top creators for formal permission | Recommended (for top 20-30 videos) |
| Have a takedown process if creator complains | Required |
| Terms of Service: clarify curated vs original content | Required |

---

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Video deleted by creator | Have 2-3 alternative videos per skill; quick swap |
| Copyright complaint | Remove immediately; replace with alternative or Soruban Original |
| Conflicting technique across videos | Expert verifies ALL curated videos for consistency |
| "Why do I need your app for YouTube links?" | Our value is the STRUCTURE (stages, progress, assessment) not just the videos. Videos are one component of the curriculum. |
| Ads in YouTube embeds | Consider YouTube Premium embed options, or prioritize Soruban Originals for most-used skills first |
